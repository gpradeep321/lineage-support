About the App

Lineage allows you to create and manage family trees with a focus on simplicity, privacy, and cultural context.

  • Build and visualize family trees
  • Add relationships and generations
  • Capture details like Gotra, Kula, and lineage
  • Beautiful dharmic-inspired themes
  • Biometric & PIN protection
  • Fully offline – your data stays on your device

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Lineage does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All information remains on your device.

2. Data Usage

The data you enter (such as names, dates, relationships, Gotra, and lineage information) is stored locally on your device solely for app functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

Lineage does not use advertising SDKs, third-party tracking, or sell user data.

The app may interact with Apple App Store or Google Play services for app distribution, in-app purchases, purchase restoration, and platform-required functionality.

4. Data Sharing

Data is only shared when you explicitly export or share it using features provided inside the app.

The app does not automatically upload or transmit your family data.

5. Security

Lineage is designed with a privacy-first architecture. Your family tree data remains stored locally on your device.

You may optionally enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or device biometrics) or PIN protection to restrict access to the app.

6. In-App Purchases

Lineage may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features or support development.

All payments are securely processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store billing systems.

We do not collect, process, or store your payment card or banking information.

7. Children's Privacy

Lineage is not directed toward children under the age required by local digital consent laws.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in future versions of the app. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
