About the App

Lineage allows you to create and manage family trees with a focus on simplicity, privacy, and cultural context.

  • Build and visualize family trees
  • Add relationships and generations
  • Capture details like Gotra, Kula, and lineage
  • Fully offline – your data stays on your device

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Lineage is designed to operate without collecting or transmitting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

Lineage does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All information remains on your device.

2. Data Usage

The data you enter (such as names, dates, and relationships) is stored locally on your device solely for app functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

Lineage does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

4. Data Sharing

Data is only shared when you explicitly export it using the app’s export feature. The app does not automatically send data anywhere.

5. Security

You may enable biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to protect access to your data within the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in future versions. Any changes will be reflected here.
